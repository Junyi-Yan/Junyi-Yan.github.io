About me

Junyi Yan is a master student supervised by Prof. Xiaoyi Lv of Xinjiang University, and is currently in her third year of graduate school. Her research interests include Graph Anomaly Detection and Graph Learning. She published ICME paper as the first author and was went to Australia to make an oral report. She has won the National Scholarship and the Outstanding Graduate Student Award of Xinjiang University. She serves as reviewer for ACM MM, ICME and DASFAA, etc.

Email: yjy@stu.xju.edu.cn / junyiiyan01@163.com
